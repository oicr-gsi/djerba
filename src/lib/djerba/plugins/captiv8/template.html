CAPTIV-8 description

The CAPTIV-8 score is computed by combining the following attributes: presence of a cancer-driving virus (VIRUSBreakend 2.13.2), loss of function mutations affecting the SWI/SNF complex (MuTect2 GATK 4.2.6.1), genome-wide Tumour Mutation Burden (MuTect2 GATK 4.2.6.1), gene expression value as measured in Transcripts per million (TPM, RSEM 1.3.3), and expression-inferred estimate of immune cell types (CIBERSORT immunedeconv 2.1.0). Additional points are included if sample is a lymph node biopsy or a colorectal cancer as indicated on the requisition form.
