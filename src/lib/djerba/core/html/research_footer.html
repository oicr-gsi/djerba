This is an automated research-use-only (RUO) report, which follow the format of our reviewed clinical reports. RUO reports are released as is, without review by our clinical interpretation team, using default pipeline settings for generation of reported metrics.

Reported variants (snvs, indels, structural variants, fusions) are not reviewed to identify potential false positives. Recipients may want to further assess identified variants by review of the variant calls and the underlying aligned sequence.

Copy number analysis with purple results in generation of multiple solutions based on a range of gamma parameters (an indicator of window size for segmentation). For reporting purposes a default solution is utilized for copy number assessment, and purity and ploidy estimates. Users may want to review the purple results to better assess the suitability of the default solution.
